About Me

My name is Edgar Granados-Perez and I am from Memphis, Tennessee. Although I was not born in the volunteer state I consider it my home. My family immigrated here from Hidalgo, Mexico when I was three. Being an immigrant has taught me many things but one of the most treasured lessons I have learned has been to always be open to learn. That is what brought me to computer science. Growing up I didn't come across many oppurtunities as my peers, one of the was having internet at home. My older brother was given a PC for his birthday and used to doodle on MS Paint all the time. Me being the annoying little brother that I was. I constantly asked him to let me use use the computer, which my brother strongly declined to do so. I would say him saying "no" was probably one of the best things to happen to me. It lead to me to research how a computer functions and what other purposes it had other than doodling. I learned about networking to solve my internet issue and learned about google docs when it released to public. A tool that helped me do countless of papers for free. Ironically enough I never figured how to use paint on windows vista to doodle, but honestly that is a small price to pay for an immense amount of knowledge.
